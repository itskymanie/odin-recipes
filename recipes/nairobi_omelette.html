The Nairobi Omelette: Omelette for days on end

Description

An Omelette tailored for its convenience. One of the first things I learned how to make as a youngin.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs or a cup of egg whites (if you et those carton eggs)
  • 1 diced roma tomato
  • 1/4 diced onion
  • 6 to 8 stems of chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 diced bell pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of italian seasoning
  • a fistful of your choice of grated cheese
  • salt and ground pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Beat eggs in a small bowl until just combined; do not overbeat.
  2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add tomato, onion, and bell pepper; season with salt and pepper. Cook and stir until onions soften, about 5 minutes.
  3. Reduce heat to medium-low and pour in eggs. Mix briefly with a spatula while shaking the pan to ensure ingredients are evenly distributed. Quickly run the spatula along edges of omelet. Sprinkle Cheddar cheese and italian seasoning over omelet.
  4. Cook, shaking the pan occasionally, until top is still wet but not runny, about 5 minutes. Use a spatula to flip eggs. I, personally like mine a little brown. "That what she said."
  5. After desired brown is achieved, fold in half and add any stuffing you'd like a quesadilla.
